Foodpanda: Customer Insights & Problem Identification

Role: Analyst · Tools: Python, Tableau, Excel

Overview

Analyzed customer behavior and satisfaction to identify root-cause problems affecting retention (delivery speed variability, vendor quality inconsistency, and price sensitivity). Approach Primary data via survey and interviews; cleaning in Excel, EDA in Python.Tableau dashboards to visualize pain points and priorities. Results Surfaced high-impact issues and mapped them to operational recommendations (quality checks, routing optimization, targeted promotions). Challenges Sampling diversity and standardizing qualitative responses. Conclusion Demonstrates analyst skill in problem identification and translating data into clear business actions.
