AI Chatbot for Campus Inquiries

Role: Team Lead & NLP Engineer

Overview

Developed an AI-powered chatbot to provide students with instant, 24/7 answers to academic questions (e.g., academic administration, university services, grade policies, courses & registration) at Paragon International University. Built on Rasa (NLU + Core) with a structured knowledge base sourced from official documents (student handbook, FAQs) and validated with faculty/staff. Accessible on Telegram and a web interface with secure access via Google OAuth.

Approach & Tools

The chatbot was engineered from the ground up to deliver instant, reliable academic information with the intelligence and flexibility students expect.

  • Core Intelligence: Built on Rasa Open Source (NLU + Core), leveraging the DIETClassifier for precise intent detection and entity extraction, supported by SpaCy embeddings and carefully designed lookup tables & synonym mappings to interpret varied student language.
  • Conversational Flow: Combined RulePolicy for predictable academic queries with Memoization for context retention, while the TED Policy acted as a robust fallback for unfamiliar conversation paths.
  • Knowledge Integration: Answers were pulled from a structured MongoDB knowledge base, meticulously curated from the official Student Handbook, academic regulations, and university office updates.
  • Multi-Platform Access: Integrated with Telegram for convenience and a secure web client using Google OAuth for identity verification, ensuring information was accessible only to legitimate students.
  • Deployment & Reliability: Deployed via Docker containers on a DigitalOcean VPS, with MobaXterm and tmux used to manage persistent, fault-tolerant services. Logging and monitoring were handled through Loguru for rapid troubleshooting.

Results & Impact

    The deployment of the chatbot transformed the way students accessed academic information at Paragon International University:

    • High User Acceptance: A TAM3-based evaluation revealed strong agreement on ease-of-use and perceived usefulness, with students highlighting the chatbot’s simplicity, speed, and accuracy.
    • Operational Efficiency: By automating answers to repetitive queries, the chatbot significantly reduced administrative workload, freeing staff to focus on higher-value tasks.
    • Accuracy & Consistency: Iterative data refinement, entity synonym mapping, and fallback policies improved answer reliability across hundreds of unique student phrasings.
    • Cross-Platform Consistency: Delivered the same trustworthy information whether accessed on Telegram or the web, maintaining a seamless experience across devices.
    • 24/7 Availability: Provided round-the-clock assistance, ensuring students could get accurate academic guidance without waiting for office hours.
